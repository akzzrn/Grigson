About Grigson Hire

Built on experience.
Driven by family.

Grigson Hire is a film and TV equipment hire company run by Ned and Billy Grigson.

We support productions with reliable kit, clear communication and a practical understanding of what Location Departments need day to day.

This has been a long time coming. Over the past year we have spent a lot of time working out how we wanted to do things properly, and why it mattered to us.

Three years ago, we lost our Dad, Neil, who many people in the Film and TV locations world may have known. Starting this company felt like the right way to carry that connection forward and build something in an industry he cared deeply about.

For us, this is about doing the basics well. Good kit, efficient turnaround, honest service and relationships built properly over time.

We're just getting started.
